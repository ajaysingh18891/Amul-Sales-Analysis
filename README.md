# Amul Dairy — Ghaziabad Territory Diagnostic Report
## Sales & Distribution Analysis | Apr–Jun 2025

---

## Project Overview

End-to-end territory diagnostic of Amul's dairy product line across Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh — conducted during a Sales & Marketing internship at GCMMF (April–June 2025).

The study covers **108 retail outlets** across **7 areas**, analysing **9,795 sales transactions** totalling **₹1.18 crore** over four months.

---

## Key Findings

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Outlets Audited | 108 |
| Total Revenue (4 months) | ₹1.18 Crore |
| Revenue Growth Mar–Jun | 25.5% |
| Star Outlets Revenue Share | 38 outlets driving 70% of revenue |
| Monthly Revenue Leakage | ₹13.13 Lakh (43% of potential) |
| Annual Recovery Opportunity | ₹1.58 Crore |
| Stockout Incidents | 804 |
| Refrigerator Impact on Sales | 656% more Paneer & Dahi sales |
| DMS Productivity Improvement | 62.5% (8 → 13 orders/day) |

---

## Analyses Conducted

| # | Analysis | Framework | Key Finding |
|---|----------|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | Retailer Segmentation | 2×2 Quadrant Matrix | 38 Star Outlets drive 70% revenue |
| 2 | Hypothesis Testing | Pearson Correlation | Fridge availability = 656% sales uplift |
| 3 | Stockout Root Cause Analysis | Pareto + Issue Tree | Systemic — no single cause >13% |
| 4 | Revenue Leakage Model | Waterfall Analysis | ₹13.13L monthly leakage identified |
| 5 | GTM Recommendations | Outlet Scoring + ROI | ₹4.27L monthly recovery projected |
| 6 | Month-on-Month Performance | Trend Analysis | 25.5% revenue growth Mar–Jun |
| 7 | Area Performance | Comparative Analysis | Vaishali highest revenue + highest stockout |
| 8 | Retailer Health Scorecard | Weighted Composite Score | Only 6/108 outlets Grade A — Healthy |
